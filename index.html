About Me

I am a Ph.D candidate at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. My research interests span the areas of circuit netlist analysis, Deep Learning (DL) and Graph Learning (GL). The objective of my research is to perform robust reverse engineering of circuit netlist of Integrated Circuits (ICs) through graph analysis and graph learning.

I received my bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from NTU in 2018.
